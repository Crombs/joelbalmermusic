About Me

Originating from Brighton, England, composing music for TV / Film / Games is my passion and my profession. I started composing for media with a short film ‘Alco-hold-up’, screened at the British Film Institute in London in 2010.

Having graduated in 2012 from the University of Leeds with first class honours in ‘Music, Multimedia and Electronics (BSc)’, I began my freelance career. I have 10 years experience with Logic Pro. My studio contains a range of instruments including Guitar, Keyboards, Bass, Double bass, Mandolin, Clarinet and Percussion.
